how to verify

Download the signed canary and verify the PGP signature yourself. Do not trust this page alone — verify the cryptographic signature.

curl -s https://drelm.org/canary.txt -o canary.txt gpg --keyserver keys.openpgp.org --recv-keys AA59B37E3EDD940B4C7E8F762189BF82F518016D gpg --verify canary.txt

If verification fails, or if the canary has not been updated in more than 60 days, assume that one or more of the canary statements may no longer be true.
